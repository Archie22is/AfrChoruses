{\sc Bawo} ndingumntwana wakho, 
Noko ndonileyo; 
Ndiyalila phambi kwakho, 
Bawo ndixolele 

{\it Bawo ndixolele 
Bawo ndixolcle
Bawo, Bawo, Bawo ndixolele.}

Iziphoso zam zininzi, 
Azinakubalwa; 
Bawo ndibekise kuWe, 
Bawo ndixolele.

Imithetho ndiyaphule,
Wena ndakudela; 
UmSindisi ndimlahlile, 
Bawo ndixolele.

Ndone ngako ukuthetha, 
Ndone nangeengcinga, 
Ndone nangemisebenzi, 
Bawo ndixolele.
 
Noko ndibe ndifanele
Ukukubulela; 
Ndone ngokukulibala, 
Bawo ndixolele.

Nqaz' izono ezininzi,
Bawo, ndizonele;
Wena ndikuhlazisile,
Bawo ndixolele.

UnguThixo Onenceba
Engazang'iphele;
Ngako ndiya lila, ndithi,
Bawo ndixolele.

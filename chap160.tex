{\sc Bawo}, ngobubele bakho
Bona umpefumlo wam;
Ndiyalila pambi kwakho,
Susa zonk'izono zam.

Ndikho ebusweni bako, 
Ungalahli izwi lam; 
Ndiphe uxolelo lwakho, 
Susa zonk'izono zam.

Nkosi yam, andikaneli, 
Yiva ukubiza kwam;
Wen' ungendibandezeli,
Susa zonk'izono zam.

Nkosi, Bawo, ndingcolile;
Hlanza intliziyo yam;
Ngezizono ndidanile,
Susa zonk'izono zam.

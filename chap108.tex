{\sc Jerusalem}, ikhaya lam,
Endilithandayo;
Wofezwa nin' umzamo wam?
Ze ndiphumle kuwe.

Obona nin' amehlo am
Loo masango mahle;
Nezitrata zegolide,
Zomzi wosinduso?

Umzi omhle kuneEden
Ongangenwa soono;
Nezihlalo zawo zinhle;
Ndikhumbula wona.

Ndiya kulangazelela,
Jerusalem entja,
Imizamo yam yophela
Ndakufika kuwe.

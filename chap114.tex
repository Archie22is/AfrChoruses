{\sc Jerusalem}, ikhaya lam, 
Endilithandayo; 
Wofezwa nin'umzamo wam?
Ze ndiphumle kuwe.

Obona nin'amehlo am
Loo masango mahle;
Nezitrata zegolide,
Zomzi wosindiso?

Umzi omhle kuneEden;
Ongangenwa soono;
Nezihlalo zawo zihle; 
Ndikhumbula wona.

Zingandidakumbisa na
Iintlungu nokufa?
Sendiyibon' iKanana
Engunaphakade.

Namabandl'aabaposti1e
Angqong' umSindisi;
Nabo bonk'abakuKristu,
Baya kuya khona.

Ndiya kulangazelela,
Jerusalem entsha;
Imizamo yam yophela
Ndakufika kuwe. 
\hfill Amen.

{\sc Ndihlatyiwe} nKosi yami,
Leli lizwi laKho;
Taru! Taru! NKosi yami,
Ndiph' uxolo lwakho.

Ma ndibon' ubuso baKho,
Bundinonelela;
Ma ndilive izwi laKho,
Lindisikelela.

Naantsi iintliziyo yami,
Ma ihlanjwe nguWe;
Koba mnandi nKosi yami
Ndakwamkelwa nguWe.
 
Ulichibi lophiliso,
Kubo abafayo; 
Ungamandla oloyiso,
Kwabaxakiweyo. 
          \hfill Amen.~~~~~~~~~

{\sc Tlo} Moya o halalelang
Moya, nnete, wa cheseho
Kena dipolong tse batang
Ditukise ka lerato.

Re be jwale ka difate
Tse hlomilweng pela metsi
Re nwesetse Moya ntate
Ka matsatsi le matsatsi.

Ka matsatsi le matsatsi
